Lemonade

$10

Ingredients

  1. Lemons: This is the tart and citrusy fruit that gives lemonade its distinctive flavor. You'll need to squeeze the juice out of the lemons.
  2. Sugar or sweetener: This adds sweetness to balance the tartness of the lemon juice here are many different sugar options you can explore, like granulated sugar, cane sugar, or liquid sugar.

Preparation methods

  • Gather your ingredients: You'll need lemons, sugar or a sweetener of your choice, and water. Lemons are the tart star of the show, and sugar balances their pucker power.
  • Wash the lemons: Give your lemons a good rinse under cool running water.
  • Juice the lemons: You can use a manual lemon squeezer or an electric juicer. If you go the manual route, consider rolling the lemons on a hard surface beforehand to loosen up the juice sacs inside.
  • Make simple syrup (optional): This combines sugar with a little water to create a concentrated sweetener that dissolves easily in your lemonade. Heat equal parts sugar and water on the stove until the sugar dissolves. Let it cool before using.
  • Mix the ingredients: In a pitcher, combine lemon juice, water, and your sweetener of choice.
  • Taste and adjust: Adjust the proportions of lemon juice, sweetener, and water to achieve your perfect balance of tart and sweet.
  • Chill and serve: For an extra refreshing drink, serve your lemonade over ice. You can also garnish it with lemon slices or fresh mint leaves.
